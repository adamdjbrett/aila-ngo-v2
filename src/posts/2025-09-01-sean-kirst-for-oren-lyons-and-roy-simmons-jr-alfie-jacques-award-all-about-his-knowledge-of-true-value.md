> Lyons and Simmons will receive the award named for their old friend at a dinner on Sept. 12, a Friday, at the Bellevue Country Club. Over the next two days, in an independent celebration, the annual [Haudenosaunee Wooden Stick Lacrosse Festival — along with the Restore Our Sacred Lake 5k Run](http://aila.ngo/the-restore-our-sacred-lake-5k-and-haudenosaunee-wooden-stick-festival-weekend-september-13th-and-14th-at-onondaga-lake-park/) — will be held at Onondaga Lake Park. A masters tournament at the festival now honors the late [Randy Hall, a Mohawk who was one of the founders of the annual gathering.](https://www.syracuse.com/kirst/2013/09/wooden_sticks_as_a_scared_lake.html)

The American Indian Law Alliance is proud to partner with the [National Institute for Law and Justice (NILJ).](https://nilj.org/) NILJ investigates cases of [Missing or Murdered Indigenous](https://nilj.org/missing-murdered-indigenous-relatives) women, girls, two-spirit folks, and all Indigenous peoples. If you are in need of NILJ's assistance please [contact them via their website](https://nilj.org/contact-us). Below is a graph that NILJ has made to help guide people through the process of submitting a case.

[![how to submit a case flow chart. The written instructions to follow](images/NILJ-submit-case.jpg)](http://aila.ngo/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/NILJ-submit-case.jpg)

Visit [nilj.org](https://nilj.org) click on [contact us](https://nilj.org/contact-us) and then scroll down to to Submit a Case for Review, input all of the relevant details and click submit.

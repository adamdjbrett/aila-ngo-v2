- Extractive Industries, Environmental Violence And Trafficking Of Indigenous Women And Girls

- You are invited to a Special Side Event, an Interactive Discussion during the 23rd Session of the UN Permanent Forum on Indigenous Issues with presentations by impacted Indigenous women and closing words by Francisco Cali Tzay, UN Special Rapporteur on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples

- Thursday April 18th from 10:00AM – 12:30 PM (during the UNPFII Closed Session)

- UN Church Center, 777 United Nations Plaza, New York, 8th Floor Conference Room

- Coffee, snacks, and interpretation in English and Spanish will be provided.

- Co-Sponsored by the International Indian Treaty Council, United Confederacy of Taino People and the International Indigenous Women’s Forum

- For information contact Rochelle Diver at [rochelle@treatycouncil.org](mailto:rochelle@treatycouncil.org)
